Content Ideas Bank

Reusable content ideas organized by pillar. Pull from this list whenever you need inspiration.

✂️ Editing Showcase Ideas

1
Before/After Split Screen
Side-by-side comparison of raw footage vs. final edit. Works for talking heads, podcasts, and B-roll. Quick to produce from any project.
Reel 15–30s Low effort
2
"Watch Me Edit This" Timelapse
Screen recording of your editing timeline sped up 10x. Add music. Show the complexity of what you do in 20 seconds.
Reel 20–30s Low effort
3
Audio Cleanup Demo
"Listen to this transformation." Play bad audio, then clean audio. Text overlay explains what you did. Perfect for podcast service promotion.
Reel 15–20s Medium effort
4
Color Grading Swipe
Use Instagram's swipe feature or a reel transition to show flat footage vs. color graded. Dramatic visual impact.
Reel or Carousel 10–15s Low effort
5
"One Cut That Changed Everything"
Show a specific editing decision that elevated a video. Explain the thinking. Positions you as strategic, not just technical.
Reel or Carousel 30–45s Medium effort

🎬 Production BTS Ideas

1
Gear Flat Lay / Setup Shot
Overhead shot of your gear laid out before a shoot. Camera, lenses, lights, audio. Caption: "Everything we need for a talking head shoot."
Photo Low effort
2
Two-Camera Setup Reveal
Show how you set up your two-camera talking head rig. This is your best seller — show why it's worth ₦50K/video.
Reel 30–45s Medium effort
3
Lighting Transformation
"Same room. Different lighting." Show how lighting changes the look completely. Before/after with setup reveal.
Reel 20–30s Medium effort
4
"Day on Set" Montage
Quick cuts from call time to wrap. Shows professionalism, energy, and the full scope of what production involves.
Reel 30–60s High effort
5
Client POV: "What It's Like to Work With Us"
Film the client experience from their perspective. Arrival, briefing, on-camera, wrap. Makes the process feel accessible.
Reel 30–45s Medium effort

💡 Education & Tips Ideas

1
"X Mistakes Killing Your Videos"
Carousel format. 3–5 common mistakes with solutions. High save rate, positions you as expert. Topics: lighting, audio, framing, editing, delivery.
Carousel 5–7 slides Medium effort
2
"How Much Does X Cost?"
Transparent pricing breakdowns. "What it costs to produce a 90-second ad." Builds trust, pre-qualifies leads, attracts serious clients.
Carousel 6–8 slides Medium effort
3
"This vs. That" Comparisons
Ring light vs. softbox. Lav mic vs. shotgun. 24fps vs. 30fps. Simple visual comparisons that educate and entertain.
Reel 15–30s Low effort
4
"What We Learned From X Project"
Post-project reflection. Share 3 lessons learned. Shows growth mindset and invites engagement from fellow creators.
Carousel or Caption Low effort
5
"The Tool We Can't Live Without"
Feature a specific tool, plugin, or technique you use daily. Quick demo. Builds authority, gets saved, invites comments.
Reel 20–30s Low effort

Content Production Workflow

A repeatable process for creating content efficiently with your in-house team.

Weekly Workflow

1
Sunday: Plan the Week
Review the calendar. Confirm which content is ready, which needs to be created. Assign tasks. 30 minutes max.
2
Monday–Tuesday: Create
Batch create the week's content. Edit 2–3 reels. Design carousels. Write captions. Get everything ready.
3
Wednesday: Review & Schedule
Final review of all content. Schedule in Meta Business Suite. Ensure captions, hashtags, and CTAs are correct.
4
Daily: Engage
15 minutes morning, 15 minutes evening. Reply to comments and DMs. Engage with relevant accounts. Build relationships.
5
Friday: Capture
Take BTS photos/videos during any client work. Build your content library. Every shoot = future content.

Content Batching Tips

From Every Client Project, Extract:
  • 1 before/after reel (editing showcase)
  • 3–5 BTS photos
  • 1 BTS video clip (15–30s)
  • 1 client testimonial quote
  • 1 lesson learned
Quick Content Ratios:
  • Reels: 60% (highest reach)
  • Carousels: 25% (highest saves)
  • Single images: 10% (brand moments)
  • Stories: Daily (engagement)
Time Investment Per Post:
  • Before/after reel: 15–30 min
  • Educational carousel: 30–45 min
  • BTS reel (from existing footage): 20–30 min
  • Photo post: 10–15 min
  • Stories: 5–10 min
